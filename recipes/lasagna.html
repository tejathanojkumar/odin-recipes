Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It is baked until bubbly and golden, making it a comforting and hearty meal.

This dish is perfect for family gatherings and special occasions, offering a delicious combination of flavors and textures in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara or meat sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add onion and garlic, and sauté until soft. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then spread ricotta mixture, and sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
