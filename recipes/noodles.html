Noodles

Description

Noodles are a versatile and popular dish enjoyed in many cuisines around the world. They can be stir-fried, boiled, or served in soups, and are often combined with vegetables, meats, and flavorful sauces.

This quick and easy noodle recipe is perfect for a satisfying meal, offering a delightful mix of textures and tastes in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (any type)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok. Add garlic and onions, saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add carrots, bell pepper, and cabbage. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until vegetables are slightly tender.
  4. Add the cooked noodles to the pan. Pour in soy sauce and chili sauce, toss everything together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
