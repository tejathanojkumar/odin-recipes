Dosa

Description

Dosa is a classic South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram) batter. It is thin, crispy, and typically served with chutney and sambar.

This savory dish is enjoyed as a breakfast or snack and is loved for its light texture and delicious flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked mixture to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight or for 8 hours until it rises.
  4. Add salt and mix well.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladleful of batter, and spread it thin.
  6. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
